\section{Relative Position}

\begin{question} \label{QKDfdfenne3rr3}
Point $A$, different from the origin, has coordinates $(a,b)$. 

Find an expression for the polar angle $\theta$ of the vector $\overrightarrow{OA} = \langle a, b \rangle$ giving the position of $A$ relative to the origin. Make sure that $0\leq \theta <2\pi$.
